About Our Team

1. Our Mission

Our mission is to build a smart home platform that enhances sustainability, security, and convenience. We aim to help homeowners reduce energy and water consumption while providing seamless control over daily household tasks through intuitive digital tools.

2. Who We Are

We are a team of students at the American University of Sharjah developing the Sustainable Smart House system as part of our coursework. Our expertise covers frontend design, backend development, database management, and interactive smart-home feature implementation.

3. What We Built

Our system includes secure login functionality, dashboards for monitoring energy and water consumption, and tools to control home devices such as AC, lights, thermostats, sprinklers, and more. Users can schedule tasks, check efficiency levels, and manage device groups across different rooms and floors.

4. Technology We Use

This platform is developed using HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, Java (Jakarta Servlets), and SQL. These technologies allow us to build a responsive, secure, and high-performance smart home environment that is both scalable and user-friendly.

6. Our Values

We prioritize sustainability, system transparency, data privacy, and reliable automation. We believe that intelligent technology should not only make life more convenient but also promote environmentally responsible behavior.

7. Project Goals

The Sustainable Smart House demonstrates full-stack development skills, covering: user authentication, device control logic, smart scheduling, data visualization, database modeling, and real-time monitoring of home efficiency. Our goal is to show how modern software can make homes smarter and greener.
